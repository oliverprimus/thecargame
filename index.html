The Car Game

How to Play

The Car Game is very simple: spot as many animals as you can before your journey is over. Good luck!

Points/Rules

Cat(7), Dog(5), Horse(4), Sheep(3), Cow (3), Pig (4), Chicken (4), Goose(4), Goat(3), Swan(3), Rabbit(3), Signs/Car Names(1).

If you spot an animal in a car, or a baby animal, hit the car/dummy button followed by the animal you saw to double your points. If you accidentally pressed the wrong button, use the wheelbarrow to go back one. To start a new game, press the hand button.
